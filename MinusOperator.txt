Minus "-" Operator

The subtraction operator "-" overloaded to allow removal of occurances of specific objects 
shared between two List<T>'s.

C# syntax example

List<T> output = List<T> list1 - List<T> list2


-Parameters-

List<T> list1
any list including custom lists of generic objects

List<T> list2
any list of compatible type with list1

-Returns-

List<T> output
a list that includes items from List1 without contents shared with List2
